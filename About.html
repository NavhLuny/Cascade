ABOUT

Tattoos, not attitudes. Piercings, not pricks. From the beginning, Cascade’s focus has been on delivering the best customer service possible within the body modification industry.

WHO ARE WE?

Cascade is one of Vietnam’s premier tattoo and piercing studios. But we’re much more than that. Our four locations across Ho Chi Minh and Ha Noi (with two more in Montreal) offer laser tattoo removal, cosmetic tattoos, and a range of uncommon brands and carefully selected products you won’t find anywhere else. With a focus on creating positive customer experiences, Cascade has become a loved lifestyle brand with a loyal following.

OUR ORIGINS

Established in 2021, Cascade emerged as a Canadian family business, started by a small group of close friends and siblings internationally in New Jersey. Over the years, Cascade studios were opened in Montreal, Ho Chi Minh and Ha Noi, and we're now exclusively in Vietnam. Throughout the past decade, Cascade has consistently been voted among the best shops in each city, with a focus on treating clients how we would like to be treated, and maintaining a family ethos amongst staff.

OUR APPROACH

Cascade takes a collaborative approach with our clients. Our aim is to be informative, helpful and inviting, guiding you and working with you through the process in receiving any one of our services. Whether it’s your first time at Cascade, or you’re a regular, we aim to ensure your experience is genuinely amazing every time.
