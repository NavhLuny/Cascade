SERVICE

We offer piercing and body jewelry, tattoos, tattoo removal, and cosmetic tattoos at our Ho Chi Minh and Ha Noi locations. Check with your nearest Cascade shop to confirm which services are in-house.

TATTOOING

We cater to the first timer, the longtime collector, someone looking for a tattoo coverup, and everyone in between. If you’re interested in a custom tattoo, all of our artists require a consultation visit prior to starting on any work. Check out our artists’ pages to get a feel for their work, and decide which tattooist you’d like to work with.

PIERCING

Get pierced by the pros in a safe and comfortable setting. With the highest standards in sanitization, all Cascade locations offer professional body piercing along with a world-class selection of body jewellery.
Due to COVID-19, piercing at our Ho Chi Minh and Ha Noi studios is strictly by appointment only (no walk-ins).

LASER TATTOO REMOVAL

You don’t have to live with bad ink, be it a botched job, the name of your ex, or a passing fad that you swore you’d love forever. Removing tattoos is now made faster, safer and easier, using technology designed to decrease the number of sessions, minimize discomfort and improve healing times. Consultations required.

AFTERCARE

Taking care of your tattoo or piercing is an important step in making your piercing or new artwork last and stay beautiful. View our instructions on how to take care of your piercing or tattoo below or view our selection of artist trusted aftercare products.
